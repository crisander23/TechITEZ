A Brief History

Established and founded by Mr. Scottie Abaño and co-founded by Mr. Crisander Dollentas, Tech IT EZ Co. is the leading tech-focused e-retailer in Philippines, with a global reach in South East Asia and the United States of America. Founded in 2025, the company offers its tens of thousands of registered users a comprehensive selection of the latest consumer electronics, entertainment, smart home and gaming products. Tech IT EZ is consistently ranked as one of the best online shopping destinations and the company regularly earns industry-leading customer service ratings. Tech IT EZ is headquartered in City of Industry, Manila. The company later on grew bigger and founded new partners which are Mr. Redien Leyba, Mr. John Louie Dela Cuesta and Mr. Cyril Limbo who greatly invested into this great company.

Our Philosophy

Tech IT EZ Co. offers competitive pricing on its unparalleled product selection, with a firm commitment to on-time order fulfillment. We empower customers to make the best purchasing decisions by offering detailed product information, peer reviews, expert opinions, product tutorials and the opportunity to network with other members of the Tech IT EZ Co. community. Customer satisfaction is Tech IT EZ's top priority, achieved by delivering superior service with our ever-present philosophy of putting the customer first.

Our Name

The name Tech IT EZ is derived for the saying Take it Easy which literally means proceed calmly and in a relaxed manner. This is to say that the founders aim to give an easy and reliable way to provide services to our customers with no hassle.
